Welcome

This blog recommends places to eat near the UNCG campus. None of these resturants take Flex, but they are all within walking distance of the Petty building. There are options for healthy foods, gluten free, or just yummy burritos! If you're looking to use Flex, there are plenty of places on campus that will accept it.

Restaurant Showcases

New York Pizza

New York Pizza is a combination pizzeria, bar, and music venue. Located on 337 Tate Street, it's a short walk from Petty and a cozy place to get a slice and a beer!

Meat Lover's Pizza

Meat Lover's featuring ham, sausage, pepperoni, ground beef, and cheese.

Sicilian Cheese Pizza

Thick crust square pizza with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.

Calzone

Baked dough turnover filled with mozzarella, ricotta, and ham

Chipotle

Casual resturant offering Mexican flare. Design-your-own burritos, bowls, and tacos! Chipotle is located at 435 Tate Street, a short walk from the Petty building. There is outdoor seating available for nice weather.

Lifestyle Bowl

No matter where you're going, lifestyle bowls can help you get there. Keto, Vegan, Vegetarian, High Protien, Gluten-free - you're free to be you with the endless options for lifestyle bowls.

Burrito Bowl

Your choice of freshly grilled meat or sofritas served in a delicious bowl with rice, beans, or fajita veggies, topped with guac, salsa, queso, sour cream, or cheese.

Burrito

Your choice of freshly grilled meat or sofritas wrapped in a warm flour tortilla with rice, beans, or fajita veggies, topped with guac, salsa, queso, sour cream, or cheese.

Sushi Republic

Short description: location, specialties, hours, and student-friendly options.

Unadon

A donburi type large bowl filled with steamed white rice, topped with fillets of eel grilled in a style known as kabayaki, similar to teriyaki.

Chicken Katsu

Also known as panko chicken or tori katsu, chicken katsu is a dish of fried chicken made with panko bread crumbs. It is prepared in a similar style to tonkatsu, fried pork cutlets.

Vegetable Tempura

Made by lightly frying various vegetables in a delicate, crispy, and airy batter. Served as either an appetizer or side dish with dipping sauce.

Quick Notes

Download the Chipotle app to earn points on each purchase! Points can be redeemed for free food. Keep an eye out for happy hour deals on pizza and beer at New York Pizza.
